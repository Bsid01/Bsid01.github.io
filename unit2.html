Other   Important   & Noteworthy   Information

The Book of Three is the beginning of a 5 book series that strikes at the hearts of those who engage in it. The content is captivating, moving, mysterious, and urges the reader to grasp for more.

  • Map
    1. shows readers where events are taking place
    2. helps readers create a mental picture in their minds
  • Author's Note
    1. Informs the reader that the world in the book is pure fiction however based off of Welsh legends
    2. The author states that there are characters that exist in those legends, but a majority of them are ones that he created himself
  • About the Author
    1. Personal background and history of the Author
    2. Gives the reader an idea of where the author got their inspiration

After reading this book, one must surely find themselves searching for the others to come.
